Why play?

Living in a disfunctional sharehouse is not fun (for most people at least!). Sharehouse Game helps solve these sharehouse problems in a fun and easy way!

Some of the problems Sharehouse Game solves:

Co-ordination problem

When was rent due again? How much did I owe you for the gas bill?

Life's too short to spend time worrying about these things!

Tragedy of the Commons

The tragedy of the commons is the name given to an age-old problem which arises when shared places, objects and consumables are overused or exhausted.

This can only happen when the objects are:

  • rival (meaning they can be used up - if you drink my milk, I'm out of milk)
  • not excludable (I can't really put a padlock on the fridge, can I?)

Free rider problem

Another age-old problem, a free rider (or freeloader) is someone who benefits from something without paying for it.

If your housemate always eats all the bread, but never goes shopping for bread, they might be freeriding!

How could a game help?

Sharehouse Game does a few things:

Points: You get points for completing tasks. Yep. Real world points.

Reminders: You'll get automated reminders for important stuff like rent and bills, before they're due!

Dashboard: Your house get's its own dashboard! At a glance you'll be able to see who's buying all the beer (or butter), who's got the most points for cleaning and who's been overseas for a month :)

Coordination tools: What about a shared shopping list, or an aggregated bill view?

We've got a few other tricks up our sleve to make your sharehouse awesome, easy and fun, but you'll have to sign up to see them :)
